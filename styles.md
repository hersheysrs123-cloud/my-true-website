# Games Page Style Guide
## Dark Theme Colours
* **Backgound:** `bg-blue-950`
* **Nav Bar Header:** `bg-indigo-900`
* **Sidebar Menu Blocks:** `bg-blue-800`
* **Accent Border Tint:** `border-blue-500`
* **Hard Structural Outlines:** `border-blue-300`
* **Text Main:** `text-sky-200`
* **Divider:** `border-indigo-600`

## Shadows
* **Box Shadow:** `shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(0,151,255,1)]`
* **Main Header Multi-Layer Shadow:** `shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(0,151,255,1),_10px_0_40px_rgba(0,151,255,1),_-10px_0_40px_rgba(0,151,255,1),_0_0_200px_rgba(0,151,255,1)]`
* **White Text Shadow:** `[text-shadow:_0_3px_10px_rgba(255,255,255,0.7)]`
* **Cyan Text Shadow (Sub-headers):** `[text-shadow:_0_4px_10px_rgba(0,255,255,0.7)]`
* **Header shadow (Indigo):** `shadow-[0_0_10px_rgba(122,29,228,0.9)]`

## Layout Rules & Presets
* **Main Typography Font:** `font-[Syne]`
* **Standard Rounded Corner:** `rounded-xl`
* **Normal Border Thickness:** `border-4`
* **Fast Transitions:** `transition-all duration-500 ease-in-out`
* **Big Slow Transitions:** `transition-all duration-1000 ease-in-out`
* **Hover Scale:** `hover:scale-125` / `hover:scale-110`
* **Button Action (Dip back)** `action:scale-50`
